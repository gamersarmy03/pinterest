BOS KNOWLEDGE PORTAL

The Board of Studies (Academic) has upgraded the BoS Knowledge Portal. It is a repository of study contents for students. The new BoS Knowledge Portal will provide a single platform for seamless course and paper wise access of LIVE and recorded lectures, syllabus, announcement, study materials, revision test papers, mock test papers, schedule, important news, student journal, other portals, etc.
